MOBILE. TABLET. DESKTOP.

Due to the steady increase in smarphones and tablets, They are dramatically changing how the general public access the internet on a daily basis. No longer is the desktop PC the primary way in which most people access the web. Mobile devices are the reigning champion. This is why it is becoming increasingly important for any modern website to be built from the ground up for mobile devices first. Luckily thanks to my work in progress BSc Hons Web Degree I know exactly how to create a fully responsive, modern, accessible website.
